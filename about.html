M.Tech Scholar · Biomedical Sciences & Engineering · IIT Guwahati

I'm a biomedical engineering student at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati with a background in pharmacy (B.Pharm, K.V.N. Naik Institute, Nashik). My work bridges pharmaceutical science and medical device engineering, with a particular focus on patient-specific implant design, 3D printing for clinical applications, biomedical instrumentation and Regulatory Affairs of Medical Devices.

Cranial3DLab was born from what I learned during my internship at Orthotech India Pvt. Ltd., in the Additive Manufacturing department, where I got hands-on exposure to a wide range of concepts in 3D printing and implant design. This website is my effort to help more people learn about patient-specific cranial implant design using open-source tools. Cranial3DLab is created purely for educational and research purposes — to make this knowledge more accessible to students, researchers, and enthusiasts interested in the field.
