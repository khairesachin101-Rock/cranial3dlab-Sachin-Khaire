✅ Completed · Frontal-Temporal-Parietal
Case 01 — Large Fronto-Temporal-Parietal Cranial Plate
A unilateral decompressive hemicraniectomy defect was reconstructed using CT segmentation in 3D Slicer, followed by mirrored-defect plate design in Meshmixer. A two-part negative mold was printed in PLA and used intraoperatively to shape a PMMA implant.
📐 Defect: F-T-P 🖨️ Material: PLA / PMMA ⏱️ Print: ~6 hrs
